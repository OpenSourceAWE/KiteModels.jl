# Project Authors
This project was started by Uwe Fechner in 2022 with the goal to make the results 
of his PhD thesis on the simulation and control of kite power systems, re-coded in a fast and modern
programming language, available to the public.

## Developers
- Uwe Fechner, Delft/ Den Haag, The Netherlands -  original author of the `KPS3` and `KPS4` kite models
- Bart van de Lint, Trondheim, Norway - author of the `RamAirKite` model

## Contributors
- Daan van Wolffelaar, Delft, The Netherlands - contributed the script `test_inertia_calculation.jl` and the related functions
